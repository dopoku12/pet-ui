FAQ

Common questions

What do you offer?

Our “Pexperts” offer one-on-one consultations with current and prospective pet owners as well as group presentations designed for veterinary, pet shelter, and pet breeding professionals.

Can you help me choose the right pet?

Yes. We will work with you and provide tailored evidence-based pet care to help determine which type of pet is right for you and your family.

Can you help with the adoption process?

Yes. We can guide you through determining which pet fits your family and home environment, completing paperwork, and bringing your new loved one home.

When will you contact me after I request a consultation?

We will contact you within 48 hours to schedule a consultation.

Are all contact form fields required?

Yes. All fields are required.
